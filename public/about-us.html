Our Mission

Holding classes over Zoom has become an unfortunate reality, introducing educators across the globe to a myriad of obstacles to adapt to. While many of these difficulties can only be solved through a return to normalcy, one significant challenge can be solved today.

That’s why we created Ease Attendance, a free and simple way to automatically track participants’ attendance within Zoom meetings. After just a few minutes of setup, you will never have to worry about manually checking if or when participants arrive again. All you will have to do is either upload a roster of students or create a class by importing participants from a Zoom meeting and our organized online dashboard will detail exactly who attended your meeting and the time at which they arrived.

We want to streamline your online classroom experience to focus on teaching as opposed to wasting valuable time to monitor attendance. In order to make Ease Attendance as efficient and useful for educators, it will be updated and refined biweekly with user feedback.
